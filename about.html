Engineering AI for Social Good.

I believe technology is most powerful when it serves humanity. My research lies at the intersection of AI, ML, and Game Theory, striving to build systems that are not just intelligent, but equitable and transparent.

The Mission

In an era where algorithms define our reality, I advocate for AI for Social Good. It's not enough to build models that predict; we must build systems that explain, protect, and empower. Whether it's enhancing education through NLP in my work on "Chalkboards to Chatbots" or ensuring fair resource distribution using Game Theory in IoT-Edge networks, my focus is on the human impact of code.

I am actively exploring Explainable AI (XAI) to demystify black-box models. My recent publication on applied AI in food quality monitoring is a step towards making decision-making processes in critical industries more transparent and reliable.

Technical Depth

  • NLP
  • Game Theory
  • Explainable AI

Research & Experience
